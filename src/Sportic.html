About Us
The PIEAS Sportic Society is the heartbeat of campus sports culture. We aim to promote a spirit of athleticism, sportsmanship, and teamwork through year-round sports events, tournaments, and fitness challenges. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just want to have some fun and stay active, our society offers the perfect platform to unleash your energy and compete with pride.

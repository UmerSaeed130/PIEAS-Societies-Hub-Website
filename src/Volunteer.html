About Us

The PIEAS Volunteer Society is a vibrant community of students dedicated to serving others and making a lasting impact. We believe in the power of collective action and the potential of every individual to contribute to positive change.

Our Mission

To cultivate a culture of volunteerism and social responsibility among PIEAS students, empowering them to become active and engaged citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in community development and social welfare, creating a more just and equitable society for all.

Volunteering Opportunities

Discover a variety of ways to get involved and make a difference. We offer diverse projects and events to match your interests and skills.

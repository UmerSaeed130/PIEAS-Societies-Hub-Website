About Us
The Islamic Society at PIEAS is a vibrant community dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth and Islamic knowledge of students. Our mission is to create an inclusive environment where students can learn, worship, and engage in activities that promote the values of Islam, foster brotherhood and sisterhood, and contribute positively to campus life. We welcome all students, regardless of background, who are eager to deepen their understanding of Islam and participate in faith-based events.
